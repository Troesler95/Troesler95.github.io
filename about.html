Who is Tyler Roesler?

Read on!

Born and raised in the small town of Sinclairville, NY, I've had a knack for technology all my life, though I didn't always know it. After high school I spent two years pursuing a music degree having a nagging itch that I wasn't where I was supposed to be. After taking Intro to Computer Science at Fredonia, my professor asked why one earth I wasn't a Computer Science major! After a lot of mulling, I decided to take the leap and haven't looked back! Since, I've gone from green behind the ears to competent Software Engineer in a matter of two years. Imagine what I can do in two more!

Why Computer Science?

Why, passion of course!

From learning HTML and CSS for fun in high school to dedicating my breaks to studying advanced features of C++ and the STL, my unique thirst for knowledge transfers well to Computer Science. Computer Science is a field that never ceases to challenge, which is much of the reason I'm drawn to it! Not only that, but I love the chase of that next big challenge, and consequently the next big solution! I spend my free time learning new languages and technologies not because I have to but because I want to!

What Do You Do For Fun?

I'm not just a robot!

When I'm not learning a new language or working on projects, I like to do all sorts of stuff for fun! I often mess around with my Audrino and my Pine64 SBC (with a few projects in the works). And yes, to fill the computer programmer trope, I play videogames from time to time! I love RPGs such as Elder Scrolls and Fallout, and of course I play Overwatch, though nowhere near competitvely! When I'm sick of staring at a screen I love to go outside and enjoy the beautiful outdoors. I often hike, fish, play tennis (however bad I may be), and take my dogs out to play!

Why Should I Hire You?

Because I'm me!

I'm a rare breed of employee. To me, Software Engineering is just plain fun! I love the chase of the solution to a problem and if I learn something new along the way, that only adds to the joy. Not only that, I have been working in customer service for years now and have developed excellent interpersonal skills which leads to less arguing and more work in a team based setting or customer interaction! If you want a team player who makes corny jokes and loves making software, I'm your guy!
